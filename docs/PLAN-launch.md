### Tier 2 — Influencers
- Football podcasts (Twee Viansen, VTBL)
- Football Twitter accounts (10k+ followers)
- EredivisieFacts-type accounts

### Tier 3 — Fan communities
- Club fan forums (Sparta fans forum!)
- Club subreddits and Facebook groups
- Let FANS discover and spread

### Tier 4 — Players
- Retired players more likely to engage
- Players from "just got a haircut" clubs (NEC, PSV)

## Taunting Features to Build

1. **WhatsApp share** — pre-filled taunting message
2. **Rivalry comparison** — side-by-side two clubs
3. **Weekly Barber Alert** — automated social post when streak is completed
4. **Instagram-optimized cards** — 1080x1080 per team

## The Viral Loop

```
See stat → Check own club → Check rival's club → Share the funnier one → Loop
```

## Timing

- **Launch:** Friday evening before a match weekend
- **Follow-up:** After every matchday with updated numbers
- **Celebrate:** When a team "gets a haircut"
- **Drama:** When a team is "bijna bij de kapper" (3-4 wins in a row)
